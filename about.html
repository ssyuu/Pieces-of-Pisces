My name is Nursyuhada Binti Shamsureli, usually called Syu in daily basis and only people closestto me called me Adda. I was born on February 2001 which had me to be a Pisces. I do not practice and trust astrological horoscope in daily life, I just loved to be called a Pisces. Yet still, I could say that I'm emotionally sensitive and prefer calm environtment.
