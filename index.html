    Life Experiences

    Volunteering


    Participating in flood volunteering is my one of an unforgetable experience. It was a grim and bleak situation, but unaffected communities who were able to help quickly began organising fundraisers and volunteer efforts to relieve the floods' impact. Properties and vehicles were wrecked, people were stranded, missing, found deceased and thousands of people are displaced.

    As I am not equipped to help with more advanced flood relief efforts as how the NGO do, what I were able to instead offer was manpower. Since I'm currently in college here in Rembau, I decided to join my batchmates and Briged Sukarelawan under UiTM's sueprvision to affected area around Negeri Sembilan to help out the victims clean their house, deliver food aid and also giving them moral support since they are losing so much due to the incident.

    Modelling


    This is actually my first out-of-comfort-zone experience on 2019. I was called by my mom's superior to be a model for her event which I had to help because of my mom. It was a brittle moment ever I would say. Had to learn those modelling things in just 2 days, yet I still made it eventhough I could not even feel myself back then. But, to be in the runway would be my last thing to do in my life.

    Modelling is totally not my thing, I would rather come as a guest rather to be in a runway and be attentive in crowds. That is because of my social anxiety that I have developed since I was in high school. Still, this is my first and last experience as a model which gaves me apprehension. It was a total fortune back then, the event was held privately in a hotel and only for small invitation which were surely be considerated.
